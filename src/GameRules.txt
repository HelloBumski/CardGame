The goal of the game is to be the Player with the most points when the game ends.
Each player starts with 5 points. Points can increase and decrease as the game progresses, but cannot be negative.
The game ends when there are no cards remaining in any draw piles.

Players gain and lose points by playing cards. Different types of cards increase/decrease points differently and may affect other players.

There are 10 types of cards:
-- Point card: The Player who plays it gains points (6-10 points).
-- Attack card: Reduces points of another player (by 5-8 points). The Player who plays it also gains points (3-4 points).
-- Freeze card: Freezes another player for one turn. Also reduces the other Player's points (by 4-6 points) The Player who plays it also gains points (1-2 points).
-- Thief card: The Player who plays it steals a card from another Player and places it in their hand. Costs 1 point to play.
-- Skip Card: End your turn immediately. You gain 1–2 points.
-- Shield Card: Play this when another player attacks you to reduce the damage you take.
-- Nope Card: Cancel the effect of another player’s ability card. A Nope Card can also cancel another Nope Card, turning that Nope into a Yes. Costs 1–2 points to play.
-- Joker Card: Choose another player. That player draws 2 cards and skips their next turn.
-- Terrorist Card: When drawn, the player who drew it loses 5–8 points.
-- See the Future Card: Look at the top 3 cards of a deck without drawing them.

Players start with 3 random cards in hand. The remaining cards start in one deck:
-- the Mixed Deck that contains a mixture of all card types

Each turn, a Player can choose one of 2 options:
-- 1. Play a card from their hand
-- 2. Draw a random card from the Mixed Deck. They do not get to play it.
